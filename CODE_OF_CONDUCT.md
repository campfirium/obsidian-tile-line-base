# Code of Conduct

TileLineBase is a small open-source project for people who want to build useful plain-text workflows in Obsidian. Please help keep the community practical, welcoming, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when asking questions, reporting bugs, or disagreeing.
- Assume good intent, especially when someone is describing a workflow that differs from yours.
- Keep feedback focused on the project, the behavior, or the tradeoff.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce issues or understand suggestions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive comments about someone's background, language, tools, or workflow.
- Posting private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove issues, discussions, comments, and pull requests that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently block participants when needed to protect the project community.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer through a private channel instead of opening a public issue.
